**Don't say:** *"We map your LinkedIn network..."*
**DO say:** *"Every warm introduction pathway, mapped and ready. No more guessing about who to ask - just strategic guidance so you can focus on building the relationship."*

**Don't say:** *"We integrate email, LinkedIn, and calls..."*
