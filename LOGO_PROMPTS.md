## CallCircle (Senior Wellness)

**Concept:** Warm, friendly, circle (connection/wellness), approachable

**Prompt:**
```
A modern minimalist logo for a senior wellness app called "CallCircle". A stylized circle made of interconnected dots forming a subtle heart shape, representing connection and care. Warm coral (#FF6B6B) and soft teal (#4ECDC4) colors. Clean, professional, trustworthy. White background. Vector style, flat design. App icon format.
```

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Coral #FF6B6B
- Secondary: Soft Teal #4ECDC4
- Background: White #FFFFFF
- Text: Dark Gray #2D3436

## SalesSherpa (B2B Sales Intelligence)

**Concept:** Guide, compass, professional, mountain/peak

**Prompt:**
```
A modern minimalist logo for a sales intelligence platform called "SalesSherpa". A stylized mountain peak with a compass needle integrated into the design, representing guidance and reaching the top. Professional navy blue (#1E3A5F) with gold accent (#F4A261). Clean, enterprise-ready. White background. Vector style, flat design. App icon format.
```

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Navy Blue #1E3A5F
- Accent: Gold #F4A261
- Secondary: Slate #64748B
- Background: White #FFFFFF
- Text: Dark #1E293B

## Usage

1. Go to OpenAI Playground or use API
